# 📊 Analysis Summary

## Problem
Daikibo Industries faced:
- High machine downtime
- Potential gender pay inequality

---

## Approach

### 1. Downtime Analysis
- Aggregated downtime by factory and device type
- Identified high-risk machines contributing to failures

### 2. Equality Analysis
- Used Excel to classify equality scores:
  - Fair (±10)
  - Unfair (>±10)
  - Highly Discriminative (>±20)

---

## Key Findings

- Shenzhen factory shows highest downtime → maintenance focus needed
- Laser cutting machines are the major source of breakdowns
- Certain job roles show strong inequality patterns → HR intervention required

---

## Business Impact

- Reduce downtime → increase production efficiency
- Address pay inequality → improve employee trust and compliance

---

## Conclusion

This analysis demonstrates how data can be used to:
- Identify operational bottlenecks
- Drive fair organizational practices
- Support decision-making with evidence
